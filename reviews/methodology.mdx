---
title: "Review Methodology & Provenance"
description: "How the review corpus on this site was collected and what guarantees apply to every quote."
---

This site's review pages draw on a corpus of **1167 real customer reviews** of Pup Choice products, exported from public product listings on Amazon and Chewy.

**Guarantees:**

1. Every quote is verbatim — no editing, paraphrasing or AI generation.
2. Every quote carries its source channel, date, reviewer first name and verified-purchase status.
3. Critical (≤3-star) reviews are included on every review page for balance.
4. Aggregate stats (counts, averages, distributions) are computed over the full corpus for the product, not just the displayed quotes.
