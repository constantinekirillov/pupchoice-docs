## Are these chews safe to give daily?

Yes — they are formulated as daily supplements. Follow the weight-based feeding chart on each label. If a dog has a medical condition or takes medication, owners should check with their veterinarian first.

## How many chews per day?

Dosing is by dog weight and printed on every jar; typical range is 1 chew (small dogs) to 3 chews (large dogs) per day. Each supplement jar contains 140 soft chews.

## Can supplements replace a vet visit?

No. Supplements support normal function. A dog with acute symptoms — blood in urine, refusal to eat, sudden lameness — needs a veterinarian, not a supplement.

## Are the treats grain-free?

The freeze-dried raw treats (salmon, turkey) are grain-free and made from raw protein plus pumpkin or sweet potato.

## Where are products made?

In the USA, by manufacturing partner BPW Lab (GMP-compliant facility, NASC quality program).

## What if my dog doesn't like them?

Palatability is the most-praised attribute across customer reviews, but every channel has its return/refund policy: Amazon and Chewy handle refunds directly; purchases on pupchoice.com are covered by the store's satisfaction policy — contact woof@pupchoice.com.

## Which product for an itchy dog?

Constant scratching without fleas usually points to the Omega-3 Skin & Coat Chews; if itching comes with digestive signs, consider the Probiotic Digestive Chews.
