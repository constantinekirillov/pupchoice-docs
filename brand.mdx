## Manufacturing

Pup Choice supplements are made in the USA by our manufacturing partner **BPW Lab**, which operates a GMP-compliant facility and participates in the National Animal Supplement Council (NASC) quality program. The GMP, NASC and Made-in-USA credentials belong to the manufacturing facility; Pup Choice is the brand that formulates and sells the finished products.

Pup Choice makes no formulation-credential claims (no veterinarian or doctoral endorsement is asserted anywhere in brand copy).
